About Valentina Tocco

My name is Valentina Tocco, and I am the managing partner of KCB Learning LLP.
At KCB, we believe strongly in nurturing independent, confident learners through carefully structured, incremental study.
Supporting children in their academic and personal growth has always been central to my professional journey, and I find immense fulfilment in watching students develop into capable, self-assured individuals.

I received a classical education, studying Latin, philosophy, maths, and physics, and later completed a Bachelor's degree in Business and Management.
